About Us

We Are Waselni

Welcome to Wasalni, the carpooling platform designed with students in mind! Our mission is to make commuting safer, more affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're looking for a ride or offering one, we connect you with fellow students who share the same route, making your journey smoother and more sustainable.

Read More

Why Choose Us

Comfortable and Convenient

Whether you're driving or hitching a ride, Wasalni offers a simple and user-friendly platform that makes finding or offering rides quick and easy.

Affordable Travel

Save on transportation costs by carpooling with others who are traveling in the same direction. It's an eco-friendly solution that helps reduce traffic and lower expenses.

Safe and Reliable

At Wasalni, we understand that safety is a top priority. Our group reservation feature allows you to travel with friends or trusted peers, ensuring peace of mind on every trip.

Happy Customers

We pride ourselves on creating a community where students feel safe, comfortable, and satisfied. Our users love the flexibility and security that Wasalni provides, and many come back for every journey.
